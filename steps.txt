Below are the steps we should follow in software engineering interview.

1) Repeat the question to make sure we understand the question and details needed.
2) Clarify what is the input and the expected output.
3) Check any assumptions with the interviewer.
4) Give one or more approach to solve the problem (without coding).
5) Discuss the tradeoff of your solutions with your interviewer.
6) Code the solution.
7) Test the solution with normal test cases.
8) Test the solution with edge test cases.
9) Optional -- There may be follow up questions, repeat the same step.
